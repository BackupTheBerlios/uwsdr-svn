The dial is turned by putting the mouse over it and pressing the left hand mouse button and moving the mouse around. The black mark will follow your mouse around as you move it, releasing the mouse button stops it from following your mouse. The tuning rate may be changed by pressing the right hand mouse button and choosing the multiplication rate from the pop-up menu.

The Frequency Keypad can also be used for changing the frequency.

It is possible to tune the SDR via the mouse and Spectrum Display by pointing and clicking with the left hand mouse button.
