The Type of the Spectrum Display can be changed by accessing the Type Menu from the Spectrum Display menu. There are three types of display, Panadpter and Waterfall.

The Panadapter types are just slightly different visual representations of the essentially the same image. It is similar in form to that shown by a spectrum analyser, with frequency being the horizontal axis and amplitude the vertical axis.

The Waterfall type is common amongst software, in it a history of the signal and its amplitude over a length of time. The horizontal axis is frequency, the vertical axis is time, and the amplitude is shown via the different colours used.
